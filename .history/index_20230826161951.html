About

Hello! I am a senior undergraduate student at Zhejiang University majoring in Computer Science, and also an honored member of the Chu Kochen Honors College. During my undergraduate studies, I am very much grateful to be advised by Prof. Yue Zhang during my research intern at Westlake University, and I spent one semester as an exchange student in Cornell University, where I worked as a research assistant in CommCollabTech. Currently I am doing a research internship in Yale University under the supervision of Prof. Arman Cohan.

I am broadly interested in artificial intelligence, linguistics and naturally natural language processing. My previous research experience includes cross-lingual summarization, multi-document summarization, and analysis of LLMs. I am open to explore everything related to AI especially in this time of LLMs. As a pseudo-polyglot, my off time is often spent on learning languages.

I will be studying in Oxford for my senior year. I am looking forward to meeting new friends and exploring new research opportunities there.
